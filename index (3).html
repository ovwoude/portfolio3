Background

About

I lead Tokenization Strategy at Ava Labs, partnering with BlackRock, Franklin Templeton, Apollo, and KKR to bring institutional capital on-chain. I bridge supply and demand: working with asset managers to tokenize securities, while building fintech distribution channels across LATAM and APAC that connect these products to where capital already moves.

At T. Rowe Price, I covered small and mid-cap banks as an equity analyst before joining their digital assets working group to explore blockchain's institutional applications. As a CFA Level 2 candidate with deep roots in traditional finance, I help institutions integrate blockchain infrastructure: not by overhauling systems that work, but by layering in what's measurably better.
